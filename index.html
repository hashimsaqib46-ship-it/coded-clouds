What makes CodedClouds different from other agencies?

OUR JOURNEY

It Started With A Dream

Five years ago, Coded Clouds didn’t start as a company, it started as a frustration. Muhammad Hashim was staring at the same problem every small business in Pakistan complained about—undocumented economy and cash-only chaos.

"He wasn’t a giant corporation, just one young guy with a laptop, a vision, and that stubborn energy."

The mission was simple: earn enough capital to launch Pakistan’s first real fintech solution for the backbone of our economy—the dukaan owners and freelancers.

Today, Coded Clouds is a quiet movement. We are sharpening our skills, building custom CRMs, and pushing the boundaries until the real product drops.

🚀 The journey hasn’t peaked yet—this is just the warm-up.
